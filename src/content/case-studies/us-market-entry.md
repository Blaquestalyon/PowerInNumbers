## The published reference deliverable

The Master’s Chair Atlanta Stage-3 Full Report is the published reference deliverable for VERIDEX BI — USA. It is treated as the canonical example of what the engine produces when applied to an operator-led US firm under the discipline of the firm’s twenty-two-gate validation standard.

The full case study — including dual-revenue modelling, the $650M TAM decomposed to a $120M Atlanta-metro SAM, P50 five-year revenue of $5.1M with explicit confidence bands, and the interstate compliance architecture (Georgia 5.19% CIT and PIT, foreign qualification, multi-state employment law) — reads at [/case-studies/masters-chair/](/case-studies/masters-chair/).
